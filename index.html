About Us

Nestled amidst the breathtaking landscapes of the Himalayas, The Grand Kalimpong offers a serene escape for travelers seeking both adventure and relaxation. Located in the charming hill station of Kalimpong, our hotel is surrounded by lush greenery and stunning mountain views, providing a perfect backdrop for your stay.

At The Grand Hotel, we pride ourselves on delivering exceptional hospitality combined with modern amenities. Our accommodations include a variety of room types, each designed to ensure comfort and tranquility.

Enjoy:

Well-Appointed Rooms: Each room features cozy furnishings, modern amenities, and stunning views of the surrounding hills. Dining Options: Savor delicious meals at our on-site restaurant, offering a menu inspired by local flavors and international cuisine.

Conveniently situated near popular attractions such as the Tharpa Choling Monastery and Mangal Dham Temple, our hotel allows easy access to explore the rich culture and history of Kalimpong. Whether you wish to hike through scenic trails or visit local markets, everything is just a short distance away.
